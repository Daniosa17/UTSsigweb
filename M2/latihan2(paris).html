Paris

Paris is a cosmopolitan area and the capital of France, with a population of 2.2 million people living in the city center and almost 12 million people in its metropolitan area - one of the largest in Europe. Paris is located in the northern part of France and is flowed by the Seine River, its reputation has become worldwide as a romantic city which also acts as a center for fashion, culture, fashion, food and world culture.
